Placement in Groups

At the beginning of the experiment, each participant will be assigned an income distribution from a set of six income distributions. The assigned distribution remains the same throughout the experiment for each participant.

After the assignment of the income distribution, you'll be placed in a group of {{ players_per_group }} participants randomly. This means that the participants in the group may or may not differ in terms of their assigned income distribution. The composition of the group remains the same throughout the experiment.
